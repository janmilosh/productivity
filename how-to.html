Instructions

Tx minutes:  Scheduled treatment time in minutes — i.e. enter 480 if you have 8 hours of treatment time on your schedule.

Time inputs:  enter clock time with a colon, as in 8:00. Use a normal 12 hour system — i.e. 1:00 should be entered for one in the afternoon. You must input a Clock in time.

Lunch inputs:  Either fill in both, or leave both blank.

Productivity:  Enter your productivity goal in percent — enter only a number without the % symbol, i.e. 90 for ninety percent

Get Clock Out:  Be sure to enter a Productivity in order to calculate this.

Get Productivity:  Be sure to enter a Clock out in order to calculate this.

Making changes:  No need to reload the page — you can type in a new number, then click the Get Productivity or Get Clock Out button again. You can clear the form for a completely fresh start.
